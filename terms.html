Terms of Value

01. Adaptability

Luxury at Rocky House is measured by utility. Our "Essentials" are curated to transition effortlessly between professional rigor and weekend leisure. We don't just sell items; we provide the flexibility for the modern individual to navigate any environment with confidence.

02. Curation & Quality

Every piece in our House undergoes a strict selection process. We prioritize materials that age with grace—whether it is the structure of our Signature Suits, the stitching of our Leisure Sets, or the technical reliability of our Solar Utilities.

03. Logistic Excellence

  • Nationwide Reach: We facilitate secure delivery across all regions in Ghana.
  • Concierge Coordination: Once your selection is finalized, our concierge manages the logistics and provides a clear delivery window.
  • Transparent Costs: Shipping fees are calculated based on weight and destination to ensure fair, direct pricing.

04. The Wholesale Tier

We empower other businesses and bulk buyers. Special pricing tiers are available for volume acquisitions across our clothing and solar categories. Wholesale partners receive prioritized fulfillment and dedicated account management.

05. Finality & Assurance

To maintain the integrity of our inventory, all items are inspected via video or in-person before shipping. Returns are accepted within 48 hours only if the item remains in original, unworn condition with all tags intact. Bespoke orders are final sale.

Our concierge is available for clarification on any of our values or procedures.
